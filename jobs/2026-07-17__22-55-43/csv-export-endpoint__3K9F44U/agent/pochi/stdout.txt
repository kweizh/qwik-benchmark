**`src/lib/csv.ts`**
- `transactionsToCsv()` emits the RFC 4180 header row `id,date,category,description,amount`, one record per row ordered by `id`, CRLF (`\r\n`) line separators, and proper quoting/escaping (fields with commas, quotes, or newlines are wrapped in `"..."` with internal `"` doubled).
